Short summary of the server:


We are a Rust/Tarkov based Minecraft server
Sandbox type gamemode
Get spawned in a big glass dome and fight for your life
Create teams of 5 using /f
Build a secret base somewhere in the dome and defend it
Mine for minerals in the deprives underground
Make money by selling minerals to shopkeepers hidden around the dome
Look for random supply drops
Avoid getting killed, because you will be temporary death-banned

If its well recieved, it will be worked on and fully realised

Our end plan for this server:


Seasons:

Server will run in seasons, every season there will be an update. For example: a new map and extra features.
Free VIP to top 3 kills at the end of a season.

Random spawns:

You randomly spawn in when you die or join the server.
When you die you get death banned for a specified amount of time.

Trading posts:

You can buy items for ₮Trollars (currency)
Traders will be in protected areas from griefing.
No high tier items at the traders.
The traders are open in specified times, night or day. (will be decided)
You can camp the traders and fight people in them so you have to be careful around them.

Supply drops:

In random locations every 4 hours (spawns high tier items that you cant craft easily)
Announces the drop to the whole server and calls out the location.

Currency:

The currency’s name is ₮Trollar.
You can buy items from trading locations and pay players for things.
You lose 50% of your money when you die.
The player that kills you gets that 50% and the other 50% stays on you.

Daily quests:

Daily quests to kill players, make items, kill monsters and more.
Quests can be obtained from a menu or a mission villager.

Buildings/caves with item respawns:

Example: Cave dungeon with spawners and respawnable ores, high tier loot at the end.
( in the future, a boss at the end )
Items that could spawn: Gold ore, Diamond ore, Enchant table, Pot stands (resources like nether warts)
Buildings could be PVP hotspots (fighting for respawns of loot)
The items can be sold for money at traders or kept in your own base/inventory.
No diamonds on the map so you would have to camp 1 or 2 specific places like a specific mine.
